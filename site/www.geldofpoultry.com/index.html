    With love for
    every chicken
    from Samarkand

    Navobod Naslli Parranda was founded in 1996 in Navobod village, Samarkand region. Over nearly three decades the farm has grown into one of Uzbekistan's leading full-cycle poultry clusters, supplying Ross-308 hatching eggs, day-old broiler chicks, complete compound feed and halal chicken meat.

    Our mission

    A full-cycle poultry cluster
    from breeding to finished products

    NNP Group controls parent stock, incubation, chick growing, feed production, slaughter, processing, packaging and delivery. This vertically integrated model keeps quality stable and traceable.
