    An authority in hatching eggs
    and day-old chicks
    for decades

    Geldof Poultry is specialised in trading hatching eggs and day-old chicks since the 1940s. Thanks to our knowhow and long-term vision, our hatchery has been succeeding for three generations in providing the best service and in building lasting relationships with our clients. Due to strict planning, 24/7 service and air-conditioned trucks for transport, we bring every order to a successful conclusion.

    Our mission

    Our way of working
    from parent stock farm
    to logistics

    At Geldof Poultry, we keep control of the entire process. From hatching to transport until the country of destination, we ensure maximum follow-up of the day-old chicks and hatching eggs.

    Parent stock farm

    To ensure the quality of our farms, we constantly invest in state-of-the art breeding farms that follow the latest technical and health developments. By ensuring that the animals reside in the best conditions, we deliver eggs of exceptional quality. In addition, systematic checks by veterinarians and analysis of samples in laboratories certify the health of the animals and ensure the safety of our finished products.

    Collection of hatching eggs

    The hatching eggs are collected twice a day automatically. Through the automatic laying nests, all eggs enter the conveyor belt. They are then visually checked during a quality control, automatically weighed and stamped with a unique farm-specific number. Now the eggs can be packed in egg trays for transport to the packing station.

    Screening and disinfection

    All farms are subject to regular analyses examined in accredited laboratories. All hatching eggs are also disinfected with certified products according to a pre-programmed process in the designated rooms. All farms are supervised by food and safety authorities to ensure safety and the products are produced according to OIE and EU legislation.

    Packing station

    Geldof Poultry has invested in the development of a specifically designed export box that ensures that hatching eggs can be shipped in optimal condition. When packing on the packing line, we subject the hatching eggs to a visual check with the utmost care.
    For a smooth tracing we label each box with the necessary information. Afterwards, we stack them automatically and wrap the boxes in foil so that they are ready for departure.

    Transport

    We transport hatching eggs and day-old chicks in our own conditioned trucks equipped with air-conditioning. Geldof Poultry’s drivers are trained to handle the eggs in the best conditions and transport them to their destination. For distant destinations, Geldof Poultry relies on partner transport companies.

    Logistics

    We have an experienced export team led by the export manager. This team is responsible for the smooth processing of the document flow from booking road & air transports to drawing up health certificates, packing lists and certificates of origin. Our export team is always in close contact with the customer to ensure the smooth transfer of official documentation.
